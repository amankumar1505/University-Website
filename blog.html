Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2021

Generally, students learn about administration at the central-office level, where most decisions are made regarding policy, operations and finances. In addition to learning about school law, principalship and school facility management, students will participate in a school district internship program essential to a full understanding of the job duties. Earning the certificate allows existing educators to advance to higher positions and have greater authority over school operations.


Before enrolling, students must possess a General Knowledge Test Cirtificate(GKTC) and show proof of relevant experience in education or a related field. Some programs require assessment of applicant skills via oral presentations, conceptual work and composition. Applicants must also provide letters of recommendation.


Teachers with administrative aspirations may achieve those goals by pursuing and earning a school administrative certificate. This program prepares educators for central-office level careers by showing them how to effectively manage all aspects of an educational facility.
